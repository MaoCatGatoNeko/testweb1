W E L C O M E ! Sisterhood's goal as an independent bookstore is to promote gender and racial equality in our selection of books. We demonstrate the diversity as well as the commonality of women's experience by providing a wide range of multi-cultural, lesbian, and bi materials, as well as titles on every aspect of women's lives.
