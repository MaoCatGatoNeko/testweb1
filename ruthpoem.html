POEM FOR RUTH

From: "Mary C. White"
(used with permission)

For Ruth Ellis, Christmas Eve, 1999

Where Peace Lives

Your friendship is a quiet dwelling place
Where peace lives.
Your comforting ways take me in
Your simple grace bids me "take my ease"
No fuss, pretense or airs live here
You welcome me just as I am
Into your open, loving heart
Where peace lives.
Where ever I am, Ruth, 
And call your name
Love washes through me
Bringing me home to myself
And the quiet dwelling place 
Where peace lives.
